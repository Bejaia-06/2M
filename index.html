About Our School

Founded in 2013, High School of Brothers Issad Ahmed and Bachir has been a cornerstone of quality education in Béjaïa.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution that nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and responsible citizenship.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, Diversity, and Lifelong Learning guide everything we do.
